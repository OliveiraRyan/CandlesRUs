    • Candle Care

    Trim the wick 1/4 inch after every use to keep the wax clean

    Allow the wax to melt to the rims before extinguishing

    Avoid burning the candle for more than 3 hours at a time

    Never leave a burning candle unattended

    Place the candle on a stable surface, heat resistant surface and away from flammable objects
