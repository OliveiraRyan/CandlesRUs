    Candle Care

    1. Trim the wick 1/4 inch after every use to keep the wax clean

    2. Allow the wax to melt to the rims before extinguishing

    3. Avoid burning the candle for more than 3 hours at a time

    4. Never leave a burning candle unattended

    5. Place the candle on a stable surface, heat resistant surface and away from flammable objects
